## 📌 Features  
- 📈 **Sales Performance Tracking** – Monitor revenue growth and profit trends.  
- 🌍 **Regional Analysis** – Identify top-performing regions and markets.  
- 🛒 **Product Insights** – Highlight best-selling products and categories.  
- 👥 **Customer Insights** – Analyze customer behavior and contribution to sales.  
- 🎯 **KPI Monitoring** – Track total sales, profit margins, and growth rate.  
- 🔎 **Interactive Visuals** – Slicers and filters for time, region, and product category.

## 📂 Dataset Information  
The dataset used for this dashboard includes:  
- **Order ID & Date** – Transaction details  
- **Customer Information** – Demographics and region  
- **Product Details** – Category, sub-category, product name  
- **Sales & Revenue** – Quantity, sales, profit, and discounts  
- **Geographic Information** – Country/Region of sales  

---

## ❓ Problem Statement  
Businesses often struggle to:  
- Track **sales trends and KPIs** in real-time  
- Identify **top-performing regions and products**  
- Understand **customer contribution** to revenue  
- Highlight **areas of loss** due to discounts or low profit margins  

---

## 💡 Solution  
The **Sales Analytics Dashboard** solves these issues by:  
- Providing **interactive visualizations** for revenue and performance  
- Highlighting **best-selling products and regions**  
- Tracking **customer segments and sales channels**  
- Enabling **data-driven decision-making** through KPIs  

---

## 📈 Key Insights (Examples)  
- Certain **regions drive the majority of sales** revenue.  
- **Top product categories** contribute the highest profit margins.  
- **Discounted items** show higher sales volume but lower profitability.  
- **Repeat customers** generate more consistent revenue streams.
